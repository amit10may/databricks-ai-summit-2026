# Event Overview, Keynotes & Themes

## The facts

- **Name:** Data + AI Summit 2026 (DAIS 2026)
- **Dates:** June 15–18, 2026 (training June 14–18)
- **Venue:** Moscone Center, San Francisco + virtual
- **Scale (per Databricks):** 30,000+ in person; tens of thousands virtual from 150+ countries; 800+ breakout sessions, hands-on training, and a hackathon focused on agentic data apps.
- **Topic spine:** Delta Lake, Apache Iceberg, Apache Spark, MLflow, Unity Catalog, Lakeflow, Databricks Apps, Databricks SQL, Lakebase — plus agentic AI, AI/BI, and open-source frameworks.

## The three themes that organize everything

Databricks framed the whole summit around governing and building for **agents**. Unity Catalog's own framing — **Control, Context, Choice** — is the clearest lens, and it recurs in the Agent Bricks and Genie launches too:

1. **Control** — Govern what agents *do*, not just what they can access. (Unity AI Gateway, Contextual Service Policies, agent tracing.)
2. **Context** — Give agents governed business meaning so they stop guessing. (Genie Ontology, Unity Catalog Semantics: Glossary, Domains, Metrics.)
3. **Choice** — No lock-in on model, format, cloud, or region. (Open formats/Iceberg v3, cross-cloud addressability, broad model support, OpenSharing.)

## Why this matters as a "state of the platform"

Two structural shifts to internalize:

- **The catalog became a runtime decision-maker.** Databricks explicitly says Unity Catalog moved "from a system of record to a runtime decision-maker for AI." Governance is now enforced *at the moment an agent acts*, not just at query-plan time.
- **Data and AI are being unified as one governed estate.** Agents, models, tools, MCP servers, and skills are now registerable, governable objects *alongside* tables and volumes in Unity Catalog. This is the architectural bet behind the whole event.

## A note on timing/maturity

Many marquee 2025 launches (Lakebase, Agent Bricks, MLflow 3, Lakeflow GA, Iceberg interop) reached **GA or major preview milestones in early-to-mid 2026**, and DAIS 2026 is where Databricks layered the *next* tier on top (e.g., Agent Bricks → full agent platform; Genie Spaces → Genie Agents). So read 2026 announcements as "maturity + agentic expansion," not "brand-new categories."
