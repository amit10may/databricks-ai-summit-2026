# What DAIS 2026 Means for New / Prospective Databricks Customers

For a net-new adopter (useful if VCreaTek advises clients evaluating Databricks).

## The pitch in one line
Databricks is positioning itself as the **unified, open, governed platform for the agentic era** — one place to run data engineering, warehousing, BI, ML/GenAI, operational apps (Lakebase), *and* govern the agents that act on all of it.

## Why a new customer should care now
- **Start governed.** Unity Catalog + Unity AI Gateway means a new customer can adopt agents with governance, lineage, cost caps, and audit from day one — instead of bolting it on later.
- **Lower lock-in than the reputation suggests.** Open Delta + Iceberg (Iceberg v3 GA), Spark Declarative Pipelines in OSS, open Metrics/OSI semantics, and OpenSharing (Linux Foundation) mean data and definitions stay portable. Easier to justify to architecture review boards.
- **Fast time-to-value on AI:** Agent Bricks ("handles the 99% infra"), Genie One (business users get an AI coworker without building anything), and Document Intelligence SQL functions reduce the build burden.
- **One platform, fewer vendors:** ingestion (Lakeflow Connect), pipelines (Lakeflow/SDP), warehouse (Databricks SQL), operational DB (Lakebase Postgres), BI/agents (Genie), ML/GenAI (MLflow 3, Mosaic AI), security (Lakewatch SIEM), CDP (CustomerLake).

## Adoption on-ramps Databricks is pushing
- **Free Edition** for learning; revamped training catalog + certifications at the summit.
- **Marketplace** (now incl. 3rd-party apps) and Partner Connect for ecosystem.
- Migration tooling and Forward Deployed Engineering for moving off legacy warehouses/lakes.

## What a new customer should weigh
- **Maturity vs hype:** many flagship 2026 capabilities are **preview** — scope a pilot, confirm GA timelines for anything production-critical.
- **Cost model:** consumption (DBUs) + new agent/token spend; model it up front and use Gateway budgets.
- **Skills:** Spark/SQL + the new declarative + agentic patterns; factor enablement.
- **Cloud choice:** cross-cloud/region addressability is now a real differentiator if multi-cloud matters.

## Net
New customers get a more complete, more open, governance-first platform than a year ago — strongest where the goal is "build governed AI agents on enterprise data without stitching ten vendors together." The main caution is preview-stage maturity on the newest, flashiest pieces.
